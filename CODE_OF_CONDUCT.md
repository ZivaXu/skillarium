# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and technically honest community. Be constructive, assume good intent, credit sources, and distinguish evidence from inference.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate misinformation, and publication of private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this standard. Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer through GitHub. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with appropriate confidentiality.

## Attribution

This concise policy is informed by the Contributor Covenant and adapted for the current size of the project.
