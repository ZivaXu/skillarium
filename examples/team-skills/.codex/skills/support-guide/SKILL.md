# Support Guide

Put the confirmed fix first. Use plain language, keep internal diagnosis out of the client-facing message, and place generic troubleshooting after the product-specific resolution.

State uncertainty when the evidence is incomplete. Do not claim that a workaround is guaranteed across every device or regional app store.
